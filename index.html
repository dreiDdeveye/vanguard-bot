FAQ

Everything you need to know about Vanguard, minting, and the ecosystem.

Vanguard is a collection of 10,000 unique, algorithmically generated NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain. Each piece features distinct visual traits and rarity levels.

Each Vanguard NFT costs 0.08 ETH to mint, plus gas fees. You can mint up to 5 per transaction.

Minting is coming soon. Follow our announcements for the launch date.

Vanguard is built on Ethereum (ERC-721). Cross-chain expansion is planned for Phase 04.

Holders get DAO governance access, $VNGRD token airdrops, staking rewards, exclusive merch, and early access to future platform features.
